PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

THIS PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT (this "Agreement") is made and entered into as of June 15, 2026 (the "Effective Date"), by and between BLUEWATER CAPITAL PARTNERS, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Buyer"), and SANDSTONE RETAIL TRUST, a Maryland real estate investment trust ("Seller").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Seller is the fee simple owner of that certain retail property commonly known as Sandstone Plaza, located at 2800 Market Street, Portland, OR 97205, and identified as Multnomah County APN R245-801-44 (the "Property"); and

WHEREAS, Seller desires to sell and Buyer desires to purchase the Property upon the terms and conditions set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 — PURCHASE PRICE AND DEPOSIT

1.1 Purchase Price. The purchase price for the Property shall be TWENTY-EIGHT MILLION DOLLARS ($28,000,000) (the "Purchase Price"), payable by wire transfer of immediately available funds at Closing.

1.2 Earnest Money. Within three (3) business days after the Effective Date, Buyer shall deposit with First American Title Insurance Company (the "Escrow Agent") earnest money in the amount of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000) (the "Earnest Money").

ARTICLE 2 — DUE DILIGENCE

2.1 Inspection Period. Buyer shall have a period of forty-five (45) days following the Effective Date (the "Inspection Period") to conduct such inspections, tests, studies, and investigations of the Property as Buyer deems appropriate. If Buyer, in its sole and absolute discretion, determines that the Property is not acceptable, Buyer may terminate this Agreement by written notice to Seller given prior to expiration of the Inspection Period, in which event the Earnest Money shall be returned to Buyer.

2.2 Title and Survey. Seller shall cause the Title Company to deliver to Buyer a current title commitment within ten (10) days after the Effective Date. Buyer shall have thirty (30) days following the Effective Date to provide Seller with written notice of any title objections.

ARTICLE 3 — FINANCING

3.1 Financing Contingency. Buyer's obligation to close is contingent upon Buyer obtaining acquisition financing on terms satisfactory to Buyer in its reasonable discretion. Buyer's financing contingency shall expire sixty (60) days after the Effective Date.

ARTICLE 4 — TENANT ESTOPPELS

4.1 Estoppel Certificates. Seller shall use commercially reasonable efforts to deliver executed tenant estoppel certificates to Buyer no later than ten (10) days prior to Closing.

ARTICLE 5 — CLOSING

5.1 Closing Date. The closing of the transaction (the "Closing") shall occur ninety (90) days after the Effective Date, or on such earlier date as the parties may mutually agree.

ARTICLE 6 — ASSIGNMENT

6.1 Assignment. Buyer may not assign its rights under this Agreement without the prior written consent of Seller, which consent may be withheld in Seller's sole discretion.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
