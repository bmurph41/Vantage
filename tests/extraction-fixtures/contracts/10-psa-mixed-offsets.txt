PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made this 3rd day of March, 2026, between MIDLAND OFFICE REIT, INC. ("Seller") and SKYLINE ACQUISITIONS LLC ("Buyer").

PROPERTY: 500 Commerce Tower, Suite 100, Kansas City, MO 64105 (APN 18-4400-007)

1. Purchase Price: The Purchase Price is $62,400,000.

2. Earnest Money. Buyer shall deposit $1,250,000 in escrow within seven (7) business days following the date hereof.

3. Due Diligence. Buyer's Due Diligence Period shall end on April 30, 2026.

4. Title. Seller shall deliver a title commitment within fifteen (15) days after the date hereof. Buyer shall have until May 10, 2026 to provide title objections.

5. Survey. Survey delivery fifteen (15) days after the date hereof; survey objections due by May 15, 2026.

6. Financing Contingency. Buyer's financing contingency shall be deemed waived at the end of the Due Diligence Period.

7. Estoppels. Tenant estoppel certificates shall be delivered to Buyer at least five (5) business days prior to Closing.

8. Closing. Closing shall occur on June 20, 2026.

9. Assignment. Buyer shall have the unrestricted right to assign this Agreement to any affiliated entity without Seller's consent.

AGREED AND ACCEPTED this 3rd day of March, 2026.

SELLER: Midland Office REIT, Inc.  /s/ Jonathan Creed, SVP Acquisitions
BUYER:  Skyline Acquisitions LLC   /s/ Amelia Park, Principal
