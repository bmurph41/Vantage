PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

This Purchase and Sale Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of August 1, 2026, between GREENFIELD INDUSTRIAL FUND II, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership ("Purchaser"), and HARBOR FREIGHT TERMINALS, INC., a California corporation ("Vendor").

1. Property. Vendor agrees to sell and Purchaser agrees to purchase that certain industrial warehouse property located at 15400 East Industrial Boulevard, Stockton, CA 95206 (APN 215-880-12) (the "Property").

2. Purchase Price. The total purchase price shall be FORTY-FIVE MILLION DOLLARS ($45,000,000).

3. Earnest Money. Purchaser shall deliver to Chicago Title Insurance Company earnest money of ONE MILLION DOLLARS ($1,000,000) by no later than August 8, 2026.

4. Due Diligence. The Inspection Period shall commence on the Effective Date and shall expire at 5:00 p.m. Pacific Time on September 15, 2026. During the Inspection Period, Purchaser may terminate this Agreement for any reason or no reason.

5. Title. Vendor shall deliver a preliminary title commitment to Purchaser on or before August 12, 2026. Purchaser shall deliver any title objections to Vendor on or before August 25, 2026.

6. Survey. Vendor shall provide an updated ALTA survey on or before August 20, 2026. Any Purchaser survey objections shall be delivered to Vendor on or before September 5, 2026.

7. Financing Contingency. Purchaser's obligation to close is subject to obtaining acquisition financing. The financing contingency shall expire on September 20, 2026.

8. Estoppels. Vendor shall deliver signed estoppel certificates from all tenants no later than October 10, 2026.

9. Closing. The Closing shall take place on October 30, 2026, at the offices of Chicago Title.

10. Assignment. Purchaser may assign this Agreement, without the consent of Vendor, to any entity controlled by or under common control with Purchaser.

EXECUTED as of the date first written above.
