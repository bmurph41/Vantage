LETTER OF INTENT
================

Date: May 1, 2026

RE: Proposed Acquisition of 4120 Oceanview Marina, 4120 Harbor Road, San Diego, CA 92106 (APN 540-210-18)

Mr. Thomas Reyes
Reyes Harbor Holdings, LLC
Seller

Dear Mr. Reyes:

Pacific Coast Marina Partners, LP ("Purchaser") is pleased to submit this non-binding letter of intent (the "LOI") to Reyes Harbor Holdings, LLC ("Seller") regarding the proposed acquisition of the above-referenced marina property (the "Property"). The material terms under which the Purchaser is prepared to proceed are as follows:

1.  Purchase Price. The purchase price for the Property shall be Twelve Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($12,500,000), payable in cash at Closing.

2.  Earnest Money. Within five (5) business days after the execution of a definitive Purchase and Sale Agreement, Purchaser shall deposit with an escrow agent mutually acceptable to both parties an earnest money deposit of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000).

3.  Due Diligence Period. Purchaser shall have sixty (60) days from the Effective Date of the PSA to conduct due diligence on the Property, including financial review, environmental Phase I, physical inspection, and title review.

4.  Closing. Closing shall occur thirty (30) days after expiration of the Due Diligence Period, subject to satisfaction of customary closing conditions.

5.  Exclusivity. Seller shall refrain from entertaining competing offers for forty-five (45) days from the date hereof.

6.  Non-Binding. Except for the exclusivity provision in Paragraph 5, this LOI is non-binding and subject to execution of a definitive Purchase and Sale Agreement.

Please indicate your acceptance by signing below and returning a copy to the undersigned no later than May 15, 2026.

Sincerely,
Pacific Coast Marina Partners, LP
By: /s/ Daniel Chen, Managing Partner
