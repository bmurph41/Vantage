ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Asset Purchase Agreement (this "Agreement"), dated as of September 10, 2026, is by and among SUNBURST HOSPITALITY GROUP, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Purchaser"), and COASTAL INN ENTERPRISES, INC., a Florida corporation ("Seller").

WHEREAS, Seller owns and operates a limited-service hotel business under the "Coastal Inn & Suites" flag, located at 2200 Atlantic Drive, Jacksonville, FL 32225;

WHEREAS, Purchaser desires to purchase from Seller, and Seller desires to sell to Purchaser, substantially all of the assets used in the operation of the business (the "Business"), on the terms set forth herein.

ARTICLE I - PURCHASE AND SALE

1.1 Assets. Seller shall sell, assign, transfer and deliver to Purchaser all of the assets, properties and rights of every kind used in the Business (the "Purchased Assets"), excluding only the Excluded Assets set forth in Schedule 1.1.

1.2 Purchase Price. The aggregate purchase price for the Purchased Assets shall be Eighteen Million Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($18,750,000), subject to working capital adjustment as set forth in Section 2.4.

1.3 Deposit. Within two (2) business days after execution of this Agreement, Purchaser shall deliver an earnest money deposit of $375,000 to escrow.

ARTICLE II - DUE DILIGENCE

2.1 Examination Period. Purchaser shall have forty (40) days after the Effective Date within which to complete its examination of the Business, the Purchased Assets, the books and records, and any other matters Purchaser deems relevant. Purchaser may terminate this Agreement at any time during such Examination Period.

2.2 Financing. Purchaser's obligation to consummate the transactions contemplated herein is contingent upon receipt of acceptable financing. The financing contingency shall expire fifty (50) days after the Effective Date.

ARTICLE III - CLOSING

3.1 Closing Date. The Closing shall take place on November 15, 2026, or such later date as the parties may agree in writing.

ARTICLE IV - ASSIGNMENT

4.1 Assignment. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party, such consent not to be unreasonably withheld.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
